Meet Matt

I'm Matt, owner and sole operator of Matt's Power Equipment Repair, based right here in Coon Rapids, Minnesota.

I've been working on small engines for years — it's what I know and what I love. Whether it's a riding mower that won't start, a snow blower that's lost its power, or a trimmer that needs a tune-up, I treat every piece of equipment like it's my own.

When you bring your equipment to me, you're not dealing with a big shop that doesn't know your name. You're getting one-on-one service from someone who takes pride in doing the job right the first time.

Honest diagnostics. Fair prices. Quality work.
