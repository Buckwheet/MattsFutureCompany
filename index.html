What We Fix

If it has a small engine,
we can fix it.

Lawn Mower Repair

Push mowers, self-propelled, riding mowers, and zero-turns. Blade sharpening, tune-ups, carburetor rebuilds, belt replacement, and complete engine overhauls. Won't start? We'll figure out why.

Snow Blower Repair

Single-stage and two-stage snow blowers. Auger and impeller repair, shear pins, carburetor cleaning, fuel system service, and pre-season tune-ups. Be ready before the first storm.

Engine Rebuilds

Complete teardown and rebuild on Briggs & Stratton, Kohler, Honda, and more. Piston rings, gaskets, valves, crankshaft work. If it's worth saving, I'll bring it back to life.

Trimmers, Chainsaws & More

String trimmers, chainsaws, leaf blowers, edgers, pressure washers, generators. All 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. Chain sharpening, fuel lines, ignition — you name it.

Call or text first so I know you're coming, but I'm flexible. We'll find a time that works.

What brands do you work on?

All major brands — Gravely, Toro, Briggs & Stratton, Kohler, Honda, Husqvarna, Craftsman, John Deere, Ariens, Stihl, and more.

How does pickup & delivery work?

I come to you with a truck and trailer, load your equipment, repair it at my shop, and deliver it back. $2.00/mile round trip.

Yes — 30-day labor warranty. If the same issue returns within 30 days, I fix it at no extra labor cost.

Absolutely. If the repair costs more than the equipment is worth, I'll tell you upfront.

Cash, check, Venmo, and Zelle. Payment due when the work is done and you're satisfied.
