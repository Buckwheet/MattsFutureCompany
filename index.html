FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 3–5 business days. Simple tune-ups and blade sharpening can often be done same-day or next-day. During peak season (spring and early winter), times may be slightly longer.

Do I need an appointment to drop off equipment?

It's best to call or text first so I know you're coming, but I'm flexible. We'll work out a time that's convenient for you.

What brands do you work on?

I service all major brands including Gravely, Toro, Briggs & Stratton, Kohler, Honda, Husqvarna, Craftsman, John Deere, Ariens, Stihl, and more. If it has a small engine, I can likely fix it.

How does pickup & delivery work?

I'll come to your location with a truck and trailer, load your equipment, bring it to my shop for repair, and deliver it back when it's done. The rate is $2.00 per mile (round trip). Great for riding mowers and heavy equipment.

Do you provide a warranty on repairs?

Yes. All labor is backed by a 30-day warranty. If the same issue comes back within 30 days of your repair, I'll make it right at no additional labor cost.

Will you tell me if it's not worth fixing?

Absolutely. If the repair cost exceeds the value of the equipment, I'll let you know upfront. I'd rather earn your trust than charge you for a repair that doesn't make sense.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash, check, Venmo, and Zelle. Payment is due when the repair is complete and you're satisfied with the work.
